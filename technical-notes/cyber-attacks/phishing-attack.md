Phishing Attack

An attacker sends a fake link pretending to be:
	•	WhatsApp
	•	Bank
	•	Courier (DHL, FedEx)
	•	Google / Apple
	•	Prize / warning message

  The goal is to:
	•	Steal passwords
	•	Steal OTPs
	•	Take over accounts

Example:
  Your WhatsApp will be blocked. Verify now:
https://whatsapp-secure-check[.]com”

 click on link → fake website opens → you enter login → attacker steals it.

 What actually gets hacked?
 Your account

 Prevention
	•	Never trust links in SMS/WhatsApp
	•	Check domain carefully
	•	Enable 2FA
	•	Use password manager (detects fake domains)
